## Full description (e.g. Play Store, 4000 chars)

**Structure:**

1. **Headline** — One sentence value proposition.
2. **What it does** — Contractor and customer reviews, dispute resolution, subscription to participate, optional integrations (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro).
3. **Key features** — E.g. verified reviews, two-sided payments, risk scores, dispute moderation, customer lookup by phone/name.
4. **Privacy & security** — Data handled securely; payments via Stripe; link to Privacy Policy and Terms.
5. **Contact** — Support email (e.g. ADMIN_EMAIL or support@yourdomain.com).

**Example opening:**

> ClientCheck helps contractors and customers participate in fair, verified reviews and payments. Check customer reputation before the job, leave or receive reviews, and resolve disputes in one place.
>
> **For contractors:** Look up customers by name or phone, see review history, manage disputes, and subscribe to access full features.
> **For customers:** Build your reputation, respond to reviews, and subscribe to support the platform.
>
> **Features:**  
> • Customer search and review lookup  
> • Two-sided reviews and dispute process  
> • Subscription (monthly/yearly)  
> • Optional integrations with field-service tools  
> • Privacy-focused; payments processed securely
>

> Terms of Service: [URL]  
> Support: [email]
