# Play Console content notes

## Suggested category
Business

## Suggested tags
contractor, risk check, customer screening, field service, payments

## Sensitive features to explain
- User-generated content is moderated
- Negative reviews require evidence
- Customers can dispute and appeal reports
- Notifications are optional and request runtime permission

## Data safety topics to review in Play Console
- Personal info
- App activity
- Photos/files if evidence uploads are enabled
- Device or other identifiers used for fraud prevention
