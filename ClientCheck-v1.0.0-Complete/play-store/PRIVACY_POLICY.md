# ClientCheck Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

ClientCheck collects information needed to provide contractor risk intelligence services, including account details, business verification data, customer search inputs, review content, dispute submissions, payment status, and device/security telemetry.

## Information we collect
- Account and profile information
- Business verification records
- Customer search inputs such as name, phone number, and address
- Reviews, evidence uploads, disputes, and moderation records
- Subscription and payment status
- Device, IP, and security/fraud-prevention signals

## How we use information
- Provide risk checks, watchlists, alerts, and review workflows
- Verify contractors and protect platform safety
- Detect fraud, abuse, and duplicate activity
- Process subscriptions and account access
- Respond to support, legal, and moderation requests

## Sharing
We may share information with service providers that help operate the platform, including cloud hosting, payment processing, email delivery, analytics, and customer support providers. We may also disclose information when legally required or to enforce our policies.

## User controls
Users can request access, correction, or deletion of account information subject to legal and safety obligations. Review and dispute records may be retained as needed for platform integrity, legal compliance, and abuse prevention.

## Data retention
We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for service delivery, safety, dispute handling, fraud prevention, billing, and legal compliance.

## Security
ClientCheck uses administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
