Short description
ClientCheck helps contractors spot risky customers before taking the job.

Full description
ClientCheck is a contractor risk intelligence app built to help service professionals make better decisions before they schedule work.

Use ClientCheck to:
- Run pre-job risk checks
- View customer payment reliability
- Track disputes and risk alerts
- Save customer watchlists
- Manage review evidence and moderation
- Monitor business insights and contractor reputation

ClientCheck is designed for plumbers, HVAC contractors, electricians, remodelers, landscapers, and other field service businesses that need better visibility into payment, cancellation, and dispute risk.

Important:
- Negative reviews require evidence
- Customers can dispute reports
- Moderation and appeals are built into the platform
- Access may require account verification
